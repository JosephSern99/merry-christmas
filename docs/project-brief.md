# Merry Christmas Website for Kayla - Project Brief

**Project Type:** Romantic Christmas Website
**Target Audience:** Kayla (girlfriend)
**Technology Stack:** Next.js/React.js

## Executive Summary

A heartfelt, romantic Christmas website celebrating Kayla - showcasing appreciation, respect, and love through a cinematic journey of 13 pictures, a personal love letter, and messages honoring her faith, passions, and purpose. The experience should feel like an ornate, interactive Christmas card that makes her feel impressed, touched, and deeply loved.

### 1. Landing Page (First Impression)
**What Happens:**
- Website opens with a **card opening animation** (unfolds like a greeting card)
- Music begins: **"A Thousand Years" by Christina Perri** (with visible play/pause controls)
- Greeting appears: "Merry Christmas, Kayla" or personalized welcome
- Simple entrance effect with rose petals and snowflakes beginning to fall

**User Sees:**
- Beautifully decorated Christmas card opening
- Twinkling lights in borders
- Elegant, festive design

**User Feels:**
- Immediate "WOW" reaction
- Anticipation and excitement
- Memories of happiness flooding back

**Duration:** 3-5 seconds

## Design System

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Golden warmth** (#D4AF37, #F4E4C1) - Beginning, love letter
- **Rosy pink** (#E8B4B8, #FFD6D1) - Middle pictures, romance
- **Deep Christmas red** (#8B0000, #C41E3A) - Final pictures, passion
- **Forest green** (#2C5F2D, #0B6623) - Accents, Christmas
- **Cream/Ivory** (#FFFDD0, #F5F5DC) - Backgrounds, text areas

**Accent Colors:**
- **Gold metallic** (#CFB53B) - Borders, flourishes, wax seal
- **Rose red** (#C21E56) - Rose petals
- **Soft white** (#FAFAFA) - Snowflakes, highlights

### Typography
**Love Letter & Notes:**
- **Font Family:** Handwritten/script style
  - Examples: "Dancing Script", "Great Vibes", "Allura", "Parisienne"
- **Size:** Responsive, grows on scroll
- **Color:** Deep burgundy or dark brown on cream background

**UI Elements (Progress, Controls):**
- **Font Family:** Elegant serif
  - Examples: "Playfair Display", "Cormorant Garamond", "Cinzel"
- **Size:** Medium, readable
- **Color:** Gold or deep red

**Picture Numbers:**
- **Style:** Elegant numerals (advent calendar aesthetic)
- **Font:** Decorative serif with ornate styling

### Visual Elements

#### Animated Elements
1. **Rose Petals + Snowflakes**
   - Fall gently throughout experience
   - **Sync:** Pulse gently with music beat
   - **Colors:** Pink/red roses, white snowflakes
   - **Density:** Subtle, not overwhelming

2. **Twinkling Lights**
   - In borders and background
   - Gentle, intermittent sparkle
   - Creates warm, festive atmosphere

3. **Parallax Effects**
   - Background layers move slower than foreground
   - Adds depth and elegance
   - Subtle, smooth transitions

#### Decorative Elements
1. **Floral Motifs**
   - Tulips and roses in borders and corners
   - Watercolor or illustrated style
   - Integrated into frames and dividers

2. **Christmas Card Flourishes**
   - Gold borders and ornate frames
   - Decorative corners and dividers
   - "Sealed with love" wax seal graphic

3. **Vintage Scrapbook Aesthetic**
   - Photo frames with decorative corners
   - Aged paper textures (subtle)
   - Elegant, timeless feel
